Global Luminescent Oil Film image based Skin friction Field Estimator(GLOF-SFE)


This is an image analysis tool for estimation of high-resolution skin friction
field from flow visualization (global luminescent oil film) images. Detail of
the method is described in following paper.

Taekjin Lee, Taku Nonomura, Keisuke Asai, and Tianshu Liu, "Linear least-squares
method for global luminescent oil film skin friction field analysis", Review of
Scientific Instruments 89, 065106 (2018).
https://doi.org/10.1063/1.5001388
